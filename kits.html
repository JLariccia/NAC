NAC Kits

Every project requires different solutions. At NAC, we believe your solutions should be as unique as your projects and that’s why we’ve customized our Crack Isolation and Waterproofing Kits to provide unique, effective solutions, in one easy step.

ECB® Kit

Crack Isolation Membrane Kit

Full floor membrane coverage provides the most effective crack isolation protection. However, the ECB Kit allows for strip applications of the membrane so you can install over hairline fractures in the substrate.

Full Product Description How it Works Features & Benefits
Kit Includes
Full Product Description

The strip application of ECB included in the kit ensures maximum fracture-free protection for tile, stone and other hard surface flooring. The ECB Kit is ideal for tiles measuring 12” or less and will protect finished floors from lateral substrate movement up to 1/4".

How it Works

The 12” x 50’ membrane is centered over the crack and installed with NAC TAC Primer for ultimate crack isolation. Proper installation is critical to performance; with one side of each tile affixed to the subfloor, the subfloor can move in-plane while ECB absorbs the stress of that movement.

Features & Benefits
  1. Designed for installations using tiles measuring 12” or less
  2. Protects finished floors from lateral substrate movement up to 1/4"
Kit Includes
  1. 12” x 50’ roll of ECB membrane
  2. Pint of NAC TAC Primer
  3. Primer brush
  4. Utility knife

Large Tile Kit

Crack Isolation Membrane Kit for Larger Tiles

Strip applications with larger tiles demands the use of a wider membrane to protect the tile from substrate movement, which is why our Large Tile Kit is ideal, providing protection for up to 1/4" of lateral substrate movement. This versatile alternative to the ECB Kit is dedicated to providing lasting solutions.

Full Product Description How it Works Features & Benefits
Kit Includes
Full Product Description

The best way to ensure maximum fracture-free protection for tile, stone and other large-format, hard surface flooring is with full floor coverage of 36” ECB membrane. However, the Large Tile Kit provides a strip application solution for larger format tile.

How it Works

The 24” x 25’ ECB anti-fracture membrane, installed over the crack with NAC TAC Primer, can accommodate tiles up to 24” x 24” and provide protection for up to 1/4" of lateral substrate movement. When applied, one side of each tile should be affixed to the subfloor so the subfloor can move in-plane while ECB absorbs the stress of that movement. Proper tile installation is critical to performance; tile should never be affixed directly to the subfloor on both sides of the membrane.

Features & Benefits
  1. Accommodates small or large tile installations (up to 24” x 24”)
  2. Protects finished floors from lateral substrate movement up to 1/4"
Kit Includes
  1. 24” x 25’ roll of ECB membrane
  2. Pint of NAC TAC Primer
  3. Primer brush
  4. Utility knife

Extreme Deck Kit

Waterproofing Kit

Combining the ultimate waterproofing forces of Strataflex and SubSeal® into one powerful kit, Extreme Deck Kit provides a durable, double layer of defense from extreme heat and cold conditions to ensure lasting protection of exterior decks.

Full Product Description How it Works Features & Benefits
Kit Includes
Full Product Description

The Extreme Deck Kit combines the accessory products SubSeal® liquid membrane, NAC TAC Primer, Seam Tape, fabric corners and a urethane sealant into a thin, low profile system of waterproofing protection. When combined with two rolls of Strataflex (sold separately), the Extreme Deck Kit will provide waterproofing protection for decks and balconies up to 300 sq. ft.

How it Works

The waterproofing capabilities of Strataflex sheet membrane and SubSeal liquid membrane combine to create a double layer for ultimate waterproofing protection. The kit also includes our various sealing accessories to ensure that every end seam and termination point is secured.

Features & Benefits
  1. Ideal for exterior decks over a living space
  2. Covers up to 300 sq. ft.
  3. Same day tile installation is possible if no flood testing is required
