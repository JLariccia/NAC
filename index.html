FLOORING SOLUTIONS

National Applied Construction (NAC) Products, Inc. is proud to offer a variety of surface-related products with solutions that are never just surface-level. With industry-celebrated innovations and unmatched service, NAC products protect your flooring investment with superior crack isolation, sound control and waterproofing solutions.

For more than 35 years, NAC has revolutionized the installation and performance of sheet applied membranes in the flooring industry. In 1983, ECB® secured industry recognition as the first self-adhering anti-fracture membrane and has since led to a variety of flooring solutions not only for crack isolation, but also for sound control, waterproofing and moisture vapor transmission.

At NAC, we believe these innovations are the foundation of world-class flooring solutions that will protect you and your building from the ground up.
