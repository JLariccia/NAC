ACCESSORIES

With any project, challenges can arise even with the best plans, which is why our NAC accessory products are designed to solve your specific issues. We offer a variety of accessories to support our floor protection systems and ensure you the strongest results.

Moisture Lock 101®

Liquid Hardener

This easy to use, pre-mixed formula hardens and seals the substrate, blocking MVT and maximizing the coverage of NAC primers to ensure a permanent bond to the substrate.

Full Product Description How it Works Features & Benefits
Full Product Description

Moisture Lock 101 is a chemical floor hardener designed for porous, cementitious substrates to harden, seal and dust-proof surfaces and help reduce MVT. As the perfect addition to your NAC flooring system, Moisture Lock 101 maximizes the coverage of NAC primers to prevent absorption into the substrate.

How it Works

As a clear, water-based liquid, Moisture Lock 101 penetrates and reacts with the free lime and calcium carbonate in concrete to fill and seal pores and capillaries, thus hardening and dust-proofing the cementitious substrates. Moisture Lock 101 maximizes coverage of NAC primers by minimizing absorption into porous, cementitious substrates, such as absorptive, moderately hard concrete, mud beds, gypsum, gypcrete, lightweight concrete and patching/leveling compounds.

Features & Benefits
  1. Maximizes coverage of NAC primers by minimizing absorption into the substrate
  2. Hardens aged, powdering concrete
  3. No VOCS, no odor
  4. Up to three coats may be applied (Allowing 24 hours between coats)

Pre-Formed Fabric Corners

Pre-Formed Inside (PFI) and Pre-Formed Outside (PFO)

NAC Pre-Formed Inside (PFI) and Pre-Formed Outside (PFO) corners are made of a lightweight, breathable fabric that can be used in conjunction with SubSeal® Liquid Waterproofing Membrane to provide an extra layer of waterproofing protection in corners.

Full Product Description How it Works Features & Benefits
Full Product Description

NAC Pre-Formed Fabric Corners provide you with unmatched protection in corner areas that are often susceptible to failure. PFI and PFO corners are commonly used for on grade or above grade interior and exterior waterproofing solutions, especially in areas above living spaces.

How it Works

PFI and PFO corners allow for substrate movement on 90-degree interior and exterior corners in walls and floors to ensure waterproofing integrity. The corners must be installed with SubSeal® Liquid Waterproof Membrane in order to become impenetrable to water intrusion.

Features & Benefits
  1. Strengthens the waterproof integrity of the corner
  2. Flexible, low profile and durable for easy installation
  3. Suitable for interior and exterior applications
  4. Tile can be installed in the same day if no flood testing is required
