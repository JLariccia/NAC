Jobsite Protection

NAC offers an array of jobsite protection products that deliver superior protection throughout the building and remodeling process. These professional grade contractor’s papers and tapes will protect a wide variety of flooring including tile, hardwood, vinyl, laminate, concrete, carpet and more. These superior jobsite protection products are here to keep your floors protected from stains, paint, spills, tools, glue and grime.

HOW IT WORKS

How Jobsite Protection Works

These products focus on the powerful forces of papers and tapes to secure every surface and seam on your project. Securing full-coverage areas or just where needed, these products keep your jobsite safe and watertight.

NAC Board

A breathable surface protector that will last for the endurance of the job and features an innovative moisture repellent technology.

NAC Board is an easy-to-use painter’s board that lays flat when rolled out, allowing floors to continue the curing process. NAC Board ensures that no paint, mud or water will get through to the surface.

  2. Easy to work with and lays flat
  3. Available in 35” x 100’ rolls
