PRODUCTS

Since the introduction of our ECB® membrane nearly 40 years ago, we’ve worked to revolutionize the way decorative flooring is installed and specified. And yet ECB, the first anti-fracture and crack isolation membrane on the market, was just the beginning for us. ECB sparked a revolution and led us to explore, develop and refine the industry in ground-breaking crack isolation, sound control, waterproofing and moisture barrier solutions.
Take a look at our offerings or share your project with us and we’ll find the best solution for you. We have curated these flooring solutions so that your projects have integrity from the ground up.

ECB ® (Elastomeric Crack Bridging) is the world’s first ever self-adhering anti-fracture membrane.
